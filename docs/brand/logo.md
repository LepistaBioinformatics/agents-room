# Logo

## Wordmark v1

**Specification:**
- Text: "Agents Room" in Inter SemiBold (600)
- Optional: 4px circle in `#C8922A` between the two words ("Agents · Room")
- Light mode color: `#171717`
- Dark mode color: `#F5F5F5`
- Minimum width: 120px
- Protection area: height of the letter "A" on all sides

---

## Favicon / App Icon

- Shape: Square with rounded corners (12px radius)
- Light background: `#C8922A`
- Dark background: `#E0A832`
- Content: 4 white circles connected in a diamond topology
- Works at sizes from 16×16 to 512×512

---

## Prohibited Uses

1. Do not rotate the logo
2. Do not apply shadow
3. Do not use on a colored background (neutral backgrounds only)
4. Do not use Lepista violet (`#7F58AF`) in the Agents Room logo
5. Do not apply gradients
6. Do not distort proportions
7. Do not combine the Agents Room accent with another Lepista product accent in the same component
