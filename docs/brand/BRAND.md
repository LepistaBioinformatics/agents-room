## Brand Pillars

| Pillar | Expression | Anti-pillar |
|---|---|---|
| Clarity | Canvas that makes the invisible visible | Not complexity for its own sake |
| Sovereignty | Offline, no account, no telemetry | Not paranoia — respect |
| Precision | Tool that does one thing well | Not a Swiss Army knife |
| Quietude | Calm interface, no intrusive notifications | Not disengagement — focus |
